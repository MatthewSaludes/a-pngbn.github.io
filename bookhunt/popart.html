Rick Riordan Isn't as Good a Writer as You Think

☆ ARTICLE OF THE MONTH

From the bad character writing to the huge plotholes, Rick Riordan's books leave much to be desired. While his books might have been your peak of literature at age 8, they aren't worth a reread at age 20, and here's why.

Number 1: he stretched the series way too long. This is undeniable. Ask any Percy Jackson fan, and they'll tell you the same thing. He was so determined to milk the cow for all its worth that he didn't notice the animal was already half-dead.
