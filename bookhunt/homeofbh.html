Books You Need to Read this Lockdown

By Arianna Panganiban

It's been a year and a half since the world turned upside down (Hamilton reference for all you Hamiltrash). And while we'd like to scroll on our phones the whole year, we do need to be more productive. Us in The Book Hunt have a solution – and have prepared a list of books for you to read and not feel completely stagnant.

If you think there won't be a genre for you, or if you think you taste is just too niche – don't worry, we got you. Even in this short 20-book list, we believe that we've covered most of the genres available. If not, feel free to check our book recommendations for specialized lists.
